# FOI Template — Sending Local Authority (Placements into Birmingham)

To: FOI Team, [Sending Local Authority]  
From: a researcher  
Subject: FOI Act 2000 request — aggregate data on placements into Birmingham supported accommodation

Dear FOI Team,

I am making this request under **section 1 of the Freedom of Information Act 2000**.

I am requesting **aggregate annual data** (not personal-level data), from **2014/15 onward** (or earliest available year), on residents placed by your authority into supported accommodation located in Birmingham.

Please provide data in **CSV** format where possible (or tabular format).

## Requested data (annual)

1. Number of residents placed by your authority into Birmingham supported accommodation.
2. Count by placement route/type:
   - commissioned block/contracted placement;
   - spot-purchased placement;
   - informal referral/signposting (if recorded);
   - other/unknown.
3. Count by reason Birmingham was selected (if recorded categories exist), for example:
   - no local availability;
   - faster availability;
   - lower cost;
   - specialist need;
   - resident choice;
   - other.
4. Count by referral source inside your authority (if recorded):
   - housing/homelessness;
   - social care/care leavers;
   - criminal justice/probation liaison;
   - hospital/discharge;
   - other.
5. Any policy or guidance documents (or excerpts) that set out when out-of-area placement into Birmingham is used rather than local placement.

## Clarifications

- Aggregate data only; no personal data requested.
- If categories differ from above, please provide your nearest equivalent categories and definitions.

## Cost-limit and narrowing instruction (FOIA s.12)

If section 12 is likely to apply, please prioritise:

1. Item (1) annual total placements into Birmingham;
2. Item (2) placement type (commissioned / spot / informal) by year;
3. Item (5) policy/guidance documents.

I am content for this request to be split into staged linked requests where that helps stay within cost limits.

Thank you.

Kind regards,  
a researcher
