# FOI Request — West Midlands Probation / HMPPS (Resettlement to Birmingham)

To: FOI Team, HMPPS / West Midlands Probation  
From: a researcher  
Subject: FOI Act 2000 request — annual aggregate placements/releases into Birmingham supported accommodation

Dear FOI Team,

I am making this request under **section 1 of the Freedom of Information Act 2000**.

I request **aggregate annual data** (not personal-level data), from **2014/15 onward** (or earliest available year), on prison/probation-related accommodation placements into Birmingham supported accommodation.

Please provide data in **CSV** format where possible (or tabular format).

## Requested data (annual)

1. Number of people released from custody or supervised by probation who were placed into supported accommodation located in Birmingham.
2. Of those, counts where the placing prison/probation case was **out of area** relative to Birmingham (where recorded).
3. Provider names used for those Birmingham placements, with annual counts by provider (where recorded).
4. Count of Birmingham placements by source route, where available:
   - prison release direct;
   - probation referral;
   - MAPPA-related route;
   - other.

## Clarifications

- Please provide aggregate totals only.
- If your data is held by operational region, establishment, or contract package rather than exactly as above, please provide your closest equivalent with field notes.
- If provider names cannot be disclosed, please provide anonymized provider IDs and counts.

## Cost-limit and narrowing instruction (FOIA s.12)

If this full request exceeds the section 12 cost limit, please prioritise:

1. Item (1) total placements by year;
2. Item (2) out-of-area count by year;
3. Item (3) provider-level counts (if feasible).

I am content for this to be treated as staged linked requests if needed.

Thank you.

Kind regards,  
a researcher
